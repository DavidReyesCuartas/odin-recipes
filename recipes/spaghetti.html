Spaghetti Carbonara

Discover how to make superb spaghetti carbonara. This cheesy pasta dish is an
italian favorite and with the right technique, you can make it perfect every time

Ingredients

  • pancetta
  • pecorino cheese
  • parmesan
  • large eggs
  • spaghetti
  • 2 plump garlic cloves
  • unsalted butter
  • sea salt and freshly ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Put a large saucepan of water on boil.
  2. Finely chop the pancetta. grate the pecorino cheese and parmesan
    and mix them together.
  3. Beat the large eggs in a medium bowl and season with a little
    freshly grated black pepper. Set everything aside.
  4. add salt to the boiling water, add the spaghetti, cook for
    10 minutes until al dente.
  5. squash the garlic cloves.
  6. fry the pancetta with the garlic and tip it in the melted butter.
  7. when the pasta is ready put it in the frying pan with the pancetta.
  8. mix most of the cheese with the eggs.
  9. pour the eggs and cheese mix in the pan. Season with a little salt.
  10. serve with a bit of cheese and a grating of black pepper.
  11. Enjoy
