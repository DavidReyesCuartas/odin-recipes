Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna recipe is made with an easy meat sauce as the base.
Layer the sauce with noodles and cheese, then bake until bubbly! This
is great for feeding a big family and freezes well, too.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spice and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • An egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
  7. Eat!
